Our Mission

The Mission of Derma Diagnose is to provide a reliable platform for the categorization of skin disorders. Our goal is to provide medical professionals with the resources they need to correctly diagnose and classify skin conditions. In order to improve diagnosis and treatment, we want to offer a quicker, more precise, and simpler method for classifying dermatological conditions utilizing modern technology.

Our Vision

Our vision at Derma Diagnose is to use machine learning prediction to enhance the classification of dermatological diseases. Through the use of machine learning algorithms like Logistic Regression, we help medical practitioners identify skin issues quickly, allowing for faster and precise treatment. Our objective is to make these technologies easy to use so that physicians can treat patients more quickly and accurately.
